Description

As an Entetainment Guide

This is a weekend event guide. It’s an App that acts as a guide for anyone who want to go for a public gig, show or party. It provides the information such as the date, time, day, price and venue. It’s got a provision of searching for a particular event in a particular town. So regardless of where you are and you don’t know where to go for a weekend outing, this App is for you.

As an Advertising Platform

This App can also be used as an advertising platform. You can advertise your entertainment packages in the features section. Any other adverts not entertainment in nature can be advertised in the section that appears at the bottom of every page.
